## Process (Brislin 1970)

1. **Forward translation** (EN → JA) by two independent bilingual translators
   working without consulting each other.
2. **Reciprocal back-translation** (JA → EN) by two further independent
   bilingual translators who have *not* seen the original English.
3. **Expert committee** meets to compare originals with back-translations,
   resolve discrepancies, and produce the final JA version.
